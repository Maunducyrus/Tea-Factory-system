About Us

Gatundu Tea Factory is committed to producing high-quality organic tea, supporting local farmers, and ensuring sustainable farming practices. Our tea is handpicked and processed with care to bring the freshest taste to your cup.

We empower farmers by offering fair prices, modern training, and a global market reach. Join us in our mission to deliver premium tea while uplifting our farming communities.
